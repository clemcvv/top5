TOP V list of the most dangerous cities in France

TOP 5 Les Beaudottes (Sevran)

• Location: Northeastern suburbs of Paris.
• Details: Sevran, particularly the Beaudottes neighborhood, is often listed among the most sensitive areas in the Île-de-France region. Drug trafficking is rampant, and tensions with law enforcement are common.

TOP 4 Le Mirail (Toulouse)

• Location: Southwest of Toulouse.
• Details: The Mirail neighborhood is often cited for its security problems, with frequent clashes between rival gangs and a strong drug trafficking presence. Unemployment and poverty are also prevalent.

TOP 3 Les Tarterêts (Corbeil-Essonnes)

• Location: Southern suburbs of Paris.
• Details: This neighborhood is known for its high crime rates and frequent gang conflicts. Les Tarterêts also struggle with integration issues and high unemployment.

TOP 2 La Castellane (Marseille)

• Location: 15th arrondissement of Marseille.
• Details: La Castellane is one of the most challenging neighborhoods in Marseille, with significant drug trafficking activity and ongoing tensions between law enforcement and residents.

TOP 1 Les Minguettes (Vénissieux)

• Location: Suburb of Lyon.
• Details: This neighborhood gained notoriety during the riots of the 1980s. Les Minguettes still face issues such as violence, drug trafficking, and high unemployment rates.
